FAQ

Good to know

Do you only build assistive tech?
No — we build for everyone. Accessibility helps more people use your product comfortably.
How do you test accessibility?
Keyboard paths, screen-reader checks (VoiceOver/NVDA), contrast audits, reduced-motion variants, and real-world tasks.
What do you deliver at the end?
Working build or prototype, docs, an improvement list, and clear next steps. No fluff.
